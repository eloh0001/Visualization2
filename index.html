Alcohol consumption in the world

"ALCOHOL"

A regulated drug. It is a colourless,volatile liquid yeast fermentation of grains, vegetables, or other fruits in which yeast or bacteria react with the sugars in the food to produce ethanol and carbon dioxide. A standard drink is any drink that contains about 14 grams of pure alcohol. It is classified as a depressant as it slows down motor processes,reaction times, and speech.

Total beer servings in each continents

There are three different alcohol type which are categorize into beer, spirit and wine. Beer servings has the highest proportion out of the alcohol type with 44.87%.

Asia is the leading beer-producing area due to large production in China but it has the second lowest proportion of 7.43% and Europe has the highest proportion of 43.94% beer servings out of all the continents.

Spirit and wine servings in each continents

Countries in Asia and North America tend to be having more spirit compared to wine servings.

Europe countries has almost the same consumption in both spirit and wine.

Other continents are more spread between wine and spirit servings in the bubble plot.

Top ten countries with the highest litres of pure alcohol

Belarus has the highest litres of pure alcohol of 14.4 followed by Lithuania and Andorra. Out of ten countries with the highest litres of pure alcohol, there are eight countries are from Europe and one each from North America and Asia.
